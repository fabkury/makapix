## 1. What happened

The Makapix Club Android app went **live in Google Play Closed Testing today (2026-07-04)** —
version 1.0.3 (versionCode 6), the same build that has been on the internal track since the
mkpx-upload flip, talking to production makapix.club. The store listing (descriptions,
screenshots, feature graphic using the new MAKAPIX wordmark) passed Google review same-day.

Thanks for the fast turnaround on `/privacy` and `/delete-account` — both were prerequisites
and both sailed through review.

## 2. The ask

Please publish a **call for testers** to the Makapix Club community — a news post on
makapix.club and wherever else you reach members (Discord, etc.). Draft copy is in §5;
adapt freely, tone is yours.

**Why it matters:** Google requires new developer accounts to run a closed test with at
least **12 testers opted in continuously for 14 days** before the app can apply for
production (public Play Store) access. Every day below 12 testers pauses the clock, so
we'd like to recruit **15–20** for buffer. The sooner the post goes out, the sooner the
public release unlocks.

## 3. How a tester joins (the exact mechanics — order matters)

1. **Join the tester group** (grants access; no email addresses collected by us):
   - Web: https://groups.google.com/g/makapix-testers → "Join group"
   - Or by email: send any message to `makapix-testers+subscribe@googlegroups.com`
     (from the address tied to their Google account)
2. **Opt in to the test:** https://play.google.com/apps/testing/club.makapix.app
   → "Become a tester" (signed in to the same Google account they use on their phone)
3. **Install** from the Play Store link that page shows.
4. **Stay opted in** (and keep the app installed) for at least 14 days — leaving the
   test early is what resets Google's clock.

If someone does step 2 before step 1 they get "app not available for this account" —
worth making the order explicit in the post. Requirements: an Android phone or tablet
(Android 5.0+, both arm64 and arm32 supported) and a Google account.

## 4. Useful context for the post

- The app is **both pillars in one binary**: the full animated pixel-art editor
  (offline-capable, no login needed to draw) and the Club (feeds, reactions, comments,
  publish, remix, layers-file attachments).
- The **player-device bar** shows up in the app already — art browsed on the phone syncs
  to registered players, which demos beautifully. Screenshots of this are in the listing.
- Feedback channels: in-app content speaks for itself, but for bugs/UX feedback point
  testers at `pub@kury.dev` (or a forum thread/Discord channel if you prefer to keep it
  community-side — your call, just tell us where so we can watch it).
- Country availability on the testing track is set from our side; if any tester reports
  the opt-in page saying "not available in your country", tell us the country and we'll
  widen the track targeting same-day.
- Please **don't link the Play Store listing directly** — during closed testing it only
  resolves for opted-in accounts. The opt-in link above is the correct entry point.
