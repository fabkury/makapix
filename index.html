The manifesto

Makapix celebrates bold experiments that feel like nostalgia from the future. We champion fast iteration, radical collaboration, and the joy of shipping weird things together.

Prototype loudly

Share your builds early and often. We give every idea a stage and the support it needs to evolve in public.

Remix culture

Every sprite, sound, and storyline is remixable. Creative credits travel with the work, keeping the community generous and fair.

Launch together

From game jam to marketplace debut, we provide the playbooks, collaborators, and momentum to ship something unforgettable.
